# Google Business Profile Setup Guide

## Step-by-Step Setup Instructions

### 1. Create Your Google Business Profile

1. Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com)
2. Sign in with your Google account (or create one)
3. Click "Manage now" or "Add your business"
4. Enter business name: **Nashville Dog Training**

### 2. Select Business Category

- **Primary Category:** Pet Trainer
- **Secondary Categories:**
  - Dog Trainer
  - Animal Behaviorist

### 3. Configure as Service Area Business

1. When asked "Do you want to add a location customers can visit?" select **No**
2. Choose "I deliver goods and services to my customers"
3. This hides your home address while showing your service areas

### 4. Define Service Areas

Add the following service areas (Nashville metro area):

1. Nashville, TN
2. Franklin, TN
3. Brentwood, TN
4. Murfreesboro, TN
5. Hendersonville, TN
6. Smyrna, TN
7. La Vergne, TN
8. Mt. Juliet, TN
9. Gallatin, TN
10. Spring Hill, TN

### 5. Add Contact Information

- Phone: [Your business phone]
- Website: [Your website URL]
- Appointment URL: [Booking link if available]

### 6. Verify Your Business

- Request postcard verification (mailed to your address, takes 5-14 days)
- Or request phone/video verification if available

Contact us today for a free consultation and discover how professional dog training can transform your relationship with your four-legged family member.

### Services List with Descriptions

Add these services in your GBP dashboard under "Services":

**Puppy Training**
Foundation training for puppies 8 weeks to 6 months. Includes potty training, bite inhibition, socialization guidance, and basic commands like sit, stay, and come.

**Basic Obedience Training**
Essential commands and manners for dogs of all ages. Covers sit, down, stay, come, heel, and polite greetings. Perfect for dogs needing structure and consistency.

**Leash Training**
Stop pulling, lunging, and reactive behavior on walks. Learn loose-leash walking techniques that make daily walks enjoyable for both you and your dog.

**Behavior Modification**
Specialized training for challenging behaviors including aggression, fear, anxiety, resource guarding, and excessive barking. Custom plans based on thorough behavior assessment.

**In-Home Training Sessions**
One-on-one training in your home where real-life distractions occur. Sessions typically 60-90 minutes with homework assignments between visits.

**Board and Train**
Intensive training program where your dog stays with our trainer for accelerated learning. Includes owner transition sessions to maintain results at home.

### FAQ Questions to Add

Add these Q&As to your GBP profile:

**Q: How long does dog training take?**
A: Most dogs show improvement within 2-4 weeks with consistent practice. Complete behavior change typically requires 6-8 weeks of training. Every dog learns at their own pace, and we customize programs to your dog's specific needs.

**Q: Do you use shock collars or punishment-based methods?**
A: No, we use positive reinforcement and science-based training methods. Research shows these approaches create longer-lasting results and maintain the trust between you and your dog.

**Q: What areas do you serve?**
A: We provide in-home dog training throughout the Nashville metro area including Franklin, Brentwood, Murfreesboro, Hendersonville, Smyrna, La Vergne, Mt. Juliet, Gallatin, and Spring Hill.

**Q: How much does dog training cost?**
A: Training packages vary based on your dog's needs and goals. We offer free consultations to assess your situation and provide transparent pricing. Contact us for a custom quote.

**Q: Can you train aggressive dogs?**
A: Yes, we specialize in behavior modification for dogs with aggression issues. We conduct thorough assessments and create safe, effective training plans. Some cases may require veterinary collaboration.

**Q: Do you offer puppy training?**
A: Absolutely! We recommend starting training as early as 8 weeks old. Early training prevents behavior problems and sets your puppy up for lifelong success.

**Q: Will you train my dog or teach me to train?**
A: Both! We train your dog during sessions while teaching you the techniques to maintain and build on that training. Owner involvement is essential for lasting results.

## Photo Guidelines

### Types of Photos to Upload

**Logo (1 photo)**
- Your business logo, square format
- Minimum 250x250 pixels

**Cover Photo (1 photo)**
- Wide format showing training in action
- Minimum 1080x608 pixels

**Training Session Photos (5-10 photos)**
- You working with dogs during actual training
- Show different breeds, sizes, ages
- Capture engagement and positive interactions
- Indoor and outdoor settings

**Before/After Results (3-5 sets)**
- Show transformation (e.g., pulling on leash vs. loose leash walking)
- Include brief caption describing the change

**Trainer Photos (2-3 photos)**
- Professional headshot
- Casual photos with dogs
- Shows you're approachable and experienced

**Location Photos (3-5 photos)**
- Training happening in client homes
- Parks and outdoor training areas
- Shows the service area environment

### Photo Naming Convention for SEO

Rename all photos before uploading with descriptive, keyword-rich filenames:

**Format:** `nashville-dog-training-[descriptor].jpg`

**Examples:**
- `nashville-dog-training-puppy-sit-command.jpg`
- `nashville-dog-training-leash-manners-brentwood.jpg`
- `nashville-dog-training-obedience-golden-retriever.jpg`
- `nashville-dog-training-behavior-modification-session.jpg`
- `nashville-dog-training-before-after-leash-pulling.jpg`
- `nashville-dog-training-franklin-in-home-session.jpg`
- `nashville-dog-training-trainer-with-labrador.jpg`

### Photo Best Practices

- Use natural lighting when possible
- Ensure dogs and people are in focus
- Avoid blurry or dark images
- Show happy, engaged dogs (not stressed)
- Get client permission before posting
- Update photos regularly (monthly if possible)
- Add new photos after successful training completions

## Ongoing GBP Management

- Add 2-4 new photos

- Update services if offerings change
- Review insights and adjust strategy

### Quarterly Tasks
- Audit profile for accuracy
- Update business description if needed
- Refresh FAQ based on common questions
- Request reviews from recent clients
