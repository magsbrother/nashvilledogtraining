### Long Description (200 words)

**For Website About Page or Premium Listings:**
```
Nashville Dog Training is Middle Tennessee's premier in-home dog training service, dedicated to helping families build better relationships with their canine companions. Founded on the belief that every dog deserves patient, science-based training, we bring professional expertise directly to your doorstep.

Our comprehensive training programs address the full spectrum of canine behavior, from puppy foundations to complex behavior modification. We specialize in potty training, crate training, basic obedience commands, leash manners, recall training, and resolving challenging behaviors including aggression, separation anxiety, fear reactivity, and excessive barking.

What sets us apart is our commitment to in-home training. Dogs learn best in the environment where they need to behave. By training in your living room, backyard, and neighborhood, we address real distractions and set your dog up for lasting success. We also emphasize owner education—teaching you the skills to maintain and build on training throughout your dog's life.

Our service area covers the entire Nashville metro region including Franklin, Brentwood, Murfreesboro, Hendersonville, Smyrna, La Vergne, Mt. Juliet, Gallatin, and Spring Hill. Whether you're bringing home your first puppy or struggling with a rescue dog's baggage, Nashville Dog Training provides the personalized support you need.

Contact us today for a complimentary consultation and take the first step toward a better-behaved dog.
```
