## Posting Best Practices

1. **Always include a photo** - Posts with images get 35% more engagement
2. **Use the CTA button** - Book Now, Learn More, Call Now, Get Quote
3. **Mention locations** - Nashville, Franklin, Brentwood, etc. for local SEO
4. **Keep it under 1500 characters** - Short and punchy wins
5. **Post between 8-10am or 6-8pm** - When people browse
6. **Respond to all comments** - Even just "Thanks!" helps engagement
